    Background

    Founded in 2025 in Naivasha, Kenya, Scene Flicks Agency is a leading platform for seamless hotel bookings, curated events, and personalized travel experiences. Our mission is to simplify the way you book accommodations and explore destinations across Africa and beyond. Whether you're a backpacker, corporate client, or family vacationer, we offer a tailored solution just for you. With an ever-expanding network of hotels, lodges, and resorts, we give you instant access to trusted listings, exclusive deals, and real-time availability. Scene Flicks Agency is not just a booking platform—it's a lifestyle hub. Explore. Discover. Book. All in one place.
