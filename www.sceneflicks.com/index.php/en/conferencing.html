Conference and Meetings

Modern conference facilities

Scene Flicks Hotel is an ideal setting for conferences and team building. We have well equipped facilities and play grounds that are amplified by the serenity and service team on the ground. Being in the outskirts of Naivasha town, we offer a quiet environment for strategic meetings, business/ social gatherings..
We currently offer the following facilities: one fully equipped ball room that can accommodate 200pax theater style; two smaller conference that can hold 50 pax each, and an executive boardroom that can sit 10pax.
We also offer top-notch professional event coordination, thorough menu planning, custom catering (including accommodation of allergies and dietary restrictions), state-of-the-art audio/visual equipment, and more!
