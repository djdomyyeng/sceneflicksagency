    💬 Frequently Asked Questions

    What’s the best area to stay in Naivasha?

    Most visitors prefer resorts along South Lake Road due to their lakefront views and proximity to major attractions.

    Are pets allowed in these hotels?

    Some hotels like Kongoni Lodge allow pets — it's best to confirm directly with the hotel.

    Can I get airport transfers from these hotels?

    Yes! Most 4-star and 5-star hotels offer airport pick-up at an extra charge.
