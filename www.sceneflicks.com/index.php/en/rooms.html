Rooms & Suites

Executive Acommodation

Our executive rooms are luxurious, and offer the ultimate comfort for the executive or holiday seeker who is looking for a serene environment together with comfort accommodation. All rooms among other things are equipped with Modern Flat Screen TVs with cable facilities, complimentary tea making facilities and extra bedding for those who may find the Naivasha weather cold at night.

The executive room only differs in theme, whereby each room is carpeted and has a circular shape with a spacious bathrooms.

Just outside the rooms, is a well-manicured garden a beautiful view from the entrance porch. You will find a Bonfire hut designed for groups and campers where fairy tales are told through the better part of the evening. It is a warm hangout joint to sip your cocktails as you enjoy the barbecue and company from friends..

The bonfire provides the necessary warmth required to resist the chilly weather of Naivasha.
